Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  1. Butter
  2. Eggs
  3. Vanilla
  4. Baking Soda
  5. Water
  6. Salt
  7. Flour
  8. Chocolate Chips

Steps

Beat butter and sugars, then beat in eggs and vanilla. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
